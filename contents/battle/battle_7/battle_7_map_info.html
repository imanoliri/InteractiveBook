Winter has settled over the Valley of Dragons, and ice wolves are wreaking havoc. While patrolling the trade routes with your squad of knights, you come across a human trading caravan under attack. Your mission: protect the caravan from the ferocious beasts and escort it safely to River City.

You're starting on the far side of the river, while wolves attack from all directions. Act quickly to reach the caravan before its guards are overwhelmed.

Remember, cavalry units deal double damage on their first attack, so position your knights to charge at the wolves instead of letting the wolves charge at them.

Optional Victory Condition: Ensure all guards survive.

Hardcore Victory Condition: Prevent any caravan members from dying.
